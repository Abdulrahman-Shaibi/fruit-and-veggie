Cautiously we return

By committing to all preventive measures you protect yourself and those around you.

  • -All customers are required to wear medical or cloth masks or what covers the nose and mouth.
  • -Commitment to a safe social distancing distance.
  • -Measuring the temperature of the employees daily and not allowing employees whose temperature exceeds [38 degrees Celsius] to Start working .
  • -Minimize employee hand contact of materials throughout the material storage journey and when -arranging materials in the market.
  • -Covering exposed goods while in transit.
